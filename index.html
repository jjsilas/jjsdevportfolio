Short Bio

I am a web developer and in the Full-stack JavaScript program at Thinkful. I enjoy coding because it allows me the opportunity to solve problems and help others to see their ideas become functional products and solutions.

Although I am comfortable working independently and remotely. I look forward to working with a great team of developers who are also enthusiastic about building useful projects.

Before starting the journey to become a developer, I worked with both reverse proxy sites and modifying our client's existing JavaScript for marketing and analytics. I love the idea of being able to completely follow the process from beginning to end. This allows me to not only to edit and maintain existing code but to develop contemporary and innovative projects as well.

When I’m not coding or working in technology, I’m frequently exploring projects with my coding group. Or training in one of my movement-related passions.

I teach Indonesian Martial Arts and ADD also known as Parkour. A few years ago I did stunt work for a movie, “The Lotus”, and I was on “American Ninja Warrior.” Periodically some of the alumni get together for training and events. It's surprising how many athletes from the show also work in the technology fields.
